The Circulatory System

The circulatory system is the system in your body that circulates blood and lymph throughout the body. The circulatory system need three main components wich are blood: a liquid to carry material, tubes to carry the blood and propulsion wich is the heart: to pump the blood. The main Function of the circulatory system is to bring mandatory substances, like oxygen, glucose, and amino acids, to all cells in the body except the cornea in your eyes. The heart send blood to your lungs to pick up more oxyegen. This system is made up of three main type of blood vessels. Wich are, the veins arteries and capillaries. Arteries carry blood away from the heart. While veins carry blood back to the heart. Cappilaries are the tine blood vessels that connect small veint tosmall arteries
