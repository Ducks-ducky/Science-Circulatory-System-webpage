Circulatory Diseases

As the website Cleveland Clinic says "Circulatory system diseases affect your heart and blood vessels and make it harder for blood to flow throughout your body. Some conditions have symptoms, but others are silent. Common symptoms include chest pain, edema, heart palpitations and shortness of breath." These diseases can cause problems with the way your heart beats, not enough blood flow, changed shape of your heart and blocked or thin blood vessels. If there is an insufficent amount of blood going to your body some parts of your body with not be able to function properly.
